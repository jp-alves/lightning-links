# ⚡ Lightning Links - The Ultimate Minimalist New Tab

A blazing fast, privacy-focused New Tab page designed for performance. Lightning Links replaces your default tab with a clean, customizable dashboard that loads instantly using standard browser technologies.

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Instant Load:** Optimized rendering ensures the page is ready the moment you open a tab.
* **Smart Preload:** Uses CSS variables to paint the layout immediately, preventing the dreaded "white flash" on startup.
* **Drag & Drop:** Intuitively reorder your shortcuts to match your workflow.
* **Fully Customizable:**
    * Adjust column count, dial size, and spacing.
    * Upload custom backgrounds (auto-optimized) or choose from presets.
* **Privacy First:** **No data leaves your device.** All shortcuts and settings are saved in your browser's `localStorage`.
* **Import/Export:** Easily backup your configuration or move it to another computer.

## 🛠 Installation (Developer Mode)

1.  Download or clone this repository.
2.  Open Chrome/Edge and navigate to `chrome://extensions`.
3.  Toggle **Developer mode** (top right corner).
4.  Click **Load unpacked**.
5.  Select the folder containing these files.
6.  Open a new tab and enjoy!

## 📦 Tech Stack

* **HTML5 & CSS3** (Grid & Flexbox)
* **Vanilla JavaScript** (ES6+)
* **Zero Dependencies** (No frameworks, no bloat)

## 🔒 Privacy Policy

Lightning Links does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All shortcuts, settings, and background images are stored locally on your device.
